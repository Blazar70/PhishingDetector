Common Signs of Phishing

  • Suspicious email addresses or domains.
  • Urgent or threatening language asking for personal information.
  • Poor grammar and spelling mistakes.
  • Unexpected attachments or links.
  • Emails claiming you won a prize you never entered a contest for.

How to Stay Safe

  • Always verify links before clicking.
  • Use multi-factor authentication for accounts.
  • Check for HTTPS (instead of just HTTP) in website URLs.
  • Be cautious of emails asking for sensitive information.
  • Regularly update your passwords.
